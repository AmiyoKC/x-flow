How x-flow works

Gotta be mindful of your running intensity to maximize cardiovascular benefits. Research shows that listening to music at a particular tempo (beats per minute or BPM) can have the effect of synchronizing the intensity of your run to your target heart rate.

An optimized run takes your heart rate to about 80% of your max heart rate. Max rate is an age dependent number and a good rule of thumb is max heart rate = 220 - age in years.

x-flow asks for your age, distance you want to run and the time you want to finish this distance in. It divides the run into 3 stages: warm-up, the middle slog and the strong finish.

  • For the warm-up phase it chooses songs based on your preference of genres and identifies songs at whose BPM is 50% of your max heart rate.
  • It gradually increases the intensity so that songs have an average of 70% of your max heart rate during the middle slog of your run.
  • Finally, you will experience a strong finish with songs reaching BPMs of 80% of you max heart rate.
